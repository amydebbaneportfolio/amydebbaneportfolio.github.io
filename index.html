My Journey


Driven by curiosity, I decided to go to a hackathon (where large groups students pull all-nighters and create fun projects) and see what all the hype was about! At Hack the North 2016, I fell in love with bringing my designs to life through code. I am fascinated by how people think, behave and work, and how technology impacts our daily lives.


Since I enjoyed it so much my goal was to eventually get an internship as a developer where I could still practice UX skills.


I eventually started an internship at Trillium Health Partners as a UX developer. However, teaching myself development was not all rainbows and buterflies. Whenever I came accross a road block I got up and played a video game! Which also happened to be a great team building activity.
